Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

For most of our specialists, a referral from your primary care physician is recommended to ensure that you are seeing the most appropriate doctor for your condition. However, some services may not require a referral. Please contact our appointments desk for more information.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance information, a list of your current medications, and any relevant medical records. For new patients, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete the necessary paperwork.

How do I schedule, reschedule, or cancel an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointments desk at (555) 123-4567, or by using our online booking tool on the Appointment page. To reschedule or cancel, please call us at least 24 hours in advance.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans. You can find a list of accepted plans on our "Patient Information" page, or you can call our billing office to verify your coverage.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records securely through our online patient portal. If you need a physical copy, you can request one from our medical records office. Please bring a valid photo ID for verification.
