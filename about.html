About Advanced Care

Our Story

Since our founding in 1998, Advanced Care has been dedicated to serving our community with a unique blend of compassion and medical excellence. We began as a small clinic with a big heart, and over the years, we have grown into a leading medical institution, but we have never lost sight of our core mission: to treat every patient like family.

Our Philosophy

We believe that true healing comes from a holistic approach that addresses not just the body, but also the mind and spirit. Our team of world-class physicians, nurses, and staff are united in their commitment to providing patient-centered care that is both comprehensive and personalized. We are not just a hospital; we are a partner in your health journey.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional and compassionate healthcare, advancing medicine through research and education, and improving the well-being of the diverse communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be a national leader in healthcare, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative research, and unwavering commitment to patient-centered care.

Our Journey

1998 - A Humble Beginning

Advanced Care is founded by a group of passionate physicians dedicated to making a difference in their community.

2005 - Expansion of Services

We expanded our facilities to include a state-of-the-art cardiology wing, bringing advanced heart care to the region.

2015 - Center of Excellence

Recognized as a Center of Excellence in Orthopedics and Neurology, a testament to our commitment to specialized care.

2023 - Embracing the Future

We continue to innovate, incorporating the latest in medical technology and research to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.
